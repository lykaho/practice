About the women

Kodul Muga Women Cutter was founded in 2018 by a group of single mothers and widows in Kochia, Kenya. The women were all struggling to provide for their families and decided to come together to fight their mutual enemy, poverty. The group members saved money that where used for school fundraising and to do catering on weddings and funerals.

The name Kodul Muga is a combination of two familynames, Kodul and Muga, that came together to fight poverty.
